College Sports Activities

Teamwork, passion, and discipline through games & athletics.

Why Sports Matter?

Sports in college life help students build teamwork, leadership, and physical fitness. Our sports programs include cricket, football, basketball, athletics, and many indoor games to promote a healthy and balanced lifestyle.

Popular Sports

Cricket

Our cricket team actively participates in inter-college tournaments, inspiring students to pursue sports with academics.

Football

Football builds strength, strategy, and speed. Students showcase their skills in friendly matches and competitions.

Basketball

We encourage students to participate in basketball tournaments, enhancing team spirit and quick decision-making.
